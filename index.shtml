Youth Ministry

The goal of the St. Peter's Youth ministry program is to, "lead teens closer to Christ." We strive to do this by offering exciting, engaging and relevant events that will help teenagers to realize how much Jesus loves them and desires to have a personal relationship with them. Whoever you are and wherever you are in your walk of faith, you have a home at St. Peter's. Be sure to check out our next event and join us, especially if you've never joined us before!


Programs:

Our youth ministry programs are based off of material from LIFE TEEN, Inc. LIFE TEEN is a Eucharistic based youth ministry program which has become an international movement. LIFE TEEN offers a 4-year curriculum for high school based on the USCCB's high school framework of catechesis and a middle school curriculum to prepare teenagers for the High School curriculum. What makes LIFE TEEN unique is it offers comprehensive Catechesis in an engaging and interactive setting.
